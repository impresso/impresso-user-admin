Researching digitised newspapers
---
Our lesson on the Ranke2 learning platform gives a first overview of the
peculiarities of digitised newspapers as historical sources.
Our tutorial on the PARTHENOS platform discusses the challenges and
opportunities researchers face when dealing with semantically enriched,
digitised newspapers.
